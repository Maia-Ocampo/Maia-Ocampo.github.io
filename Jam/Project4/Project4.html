Frutiger Aero

Role: UI & 3D Artist

Ready, Set, Launch! Frutiger Aero is a 3rd person 3D platformer where you find yourself as a fish! Fling yourself from bubble to bubble all the while dodging obstacles. The game itself was initially made for a jam that focused on the Frutiger Asthetic. Our asthetic vision consisted of focusing on that era and wished to focus on the use of bubbles and being under water. As an artist for the project, I was given the task to replicate early 2000's era style buttons for game's UI. I was also in charge of creating the bubble shader, creating and coding in Unity engine to replicate the frutiger era, soap bubble vision.
