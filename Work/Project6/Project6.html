Frog Ninja: Croaked In Shadow

Role: Producer

Frog Ninja is a set to be released stealth action game where the player takes on the role as the "Frog Ninja". With myself as the producer, my main job is to keep production up to speed and the team accountable to the tasks that are needed to be completed! Using softwares such as Jira, Figma and the microsoft suite, I strategize the realistic pacing of our team of 7 and document the dynamics of the team and tasks as the project progresses.
