Princess Starshine vs The Demons of Hell

Role: Production Manager

Princess Starshine vs. The Demons of Hell is a vibrant bullet-heaven action game where players enhance their magical arsenal with whimsical weapons and upgrades, following the journey of Princess Starshine, Virgil, and the servants of hell.

As the Production Manager for the Game Analysis and Design Interest Group, I was responsible for overseeing the successful completion of the game's fall production. My role included coordinating with multidisciplinary teams and directors to ensure smooth collaboration and workflow. Additionally, I introduced new documentation processes to improve visibility into team progress and enhance communication across internal departments.
