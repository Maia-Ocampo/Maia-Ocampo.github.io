Tanbii

Role: UX/UI Intern

Tanbii is a serious game mobile app focused on instilling green habits! As you play through the game and account for your efforts towards sustainability, the game offers the player the chanve to win rewards that are translated to real life! The company itself is a small start up and is in a stage of development requiring lots of feature and design iteration. As a UI UX intern, I work closely with both leadership and internal stakeholders to anticipate user needs and meaningful convey them.
